Projects

Sample of my past and current work. The coding is done in Python and reporting in PowerBI.

Market demand forecasting for private transfers company

Using past booking data developed demand forecast model to predict number of expected services 8 weeks in advance, in order to give the company enough time to hire the drivers. Project required a lot of feature engineering and data transformations, and final model was able to predict with maximum deviation of 10% from true demand.

Product Recommendations using Google Pagerank

Developed predictive model for an e-shop which shows list of recommended products at check-in based on individual customer shopping history and the list of products customer was likely to forget at check-out. Model adapted from Google pagerank algorithm. Find out more here

Customer segmentation for hospitality industry

Conducted customer analysis using k-means clustering. Identified customer behaviour patterns and produced a report on customer characteristics for each segment which identified opportunities for target marketing.

PowerBI Dashboard for a global retail company

Built business dashboard focusing on Sales & GP Analysis and Product and Market analysis identifying key KPI metrics. Project included building ETL Pipeline, defining dimensional schema and creating interactive dashboard. Find out more here
